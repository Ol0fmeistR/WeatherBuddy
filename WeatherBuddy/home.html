Your Weather Buddy

It is always cool to know what the weather
will be like at any time. Whether it is your own locality,
or some remote corner of the world, "Your Weather Buddy"
lets you know the weather for that place accurately, so that you don't
find yourself stuck in unfavorable conditions. Do register with us if you want to
contribute more to the project as it is still in it's infancy and we would love your input.
